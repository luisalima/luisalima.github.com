---
title: "Build the scaffolding, not the system"
pubDatetime: 2026-05-20T12:00:00.000Z
description: "In AI-assisted development the job shifts from building system pieces directly to designing the tools and building blocks that let the team deliver."
kind: note
tags: ["ai-assisted-coding", "software-architecture"]
---

As software engineers, our job is to decompose a system into pieces, then to build out those pieces. We think from the ground up in terms of direct instructions. When first transitioning to AI-assisted development we tend to keep that modus operandi.

But to be successful, the job gets closer to that of a (good) technical manager enabling a team at a higher level: design the system components that do not belong to the end system, but rather the supporting tools and building blocks that enable the team to deliver.
